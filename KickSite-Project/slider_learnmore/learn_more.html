SENSEI
A Karate Sensei plays a pivotal role in shaping not only the martial skills of their students but also their character, helping them become well-rounded individuals both on and off the training mat. typically a black belt holder, often with many years of training and practical experience in Karate. Beyond technical proficiency, a Sensei embodies the core principles of martial arts, including discipline, respect, humility, and a commitment to continuous self-improvement.
