KARATE

The Japanese Art Of Self Defense

Karate is a traditional Japanese martial art that originated on the island of Okinawa and later spread globally. The term "karate" translates to "empty hand" in Japanese, highlighting its focus on unarmed combat techniques. Practitioners use punches, kicks, knee strikes, elbow strikes, and open-hand techniques for self-defense. Karate has various styles, each with unique techniques and emphases, such as Shotokan, Shito-ryu, Goju-ryu, and Wado-ryu. Overall, karate offers physical fitness, mental discipline, and self-defense skills to its practitioners.
